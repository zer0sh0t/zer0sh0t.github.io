**My definition of intelligence**: the ability of an entity to acquire new skills from observing new data and generalizing these skills to new domains.

|    |human intelligence|arificial intelligence|
|---|:---:|:---:|
|**source of intelligence**|biological neural nets(inside brain)|artificial neural nets(right now)|
|**learning process**|we don't fully understand yet|backpropagation and optimization|
|**number of synapses**|>1 trillion|175 billion(current highest)|
|**degree of generalization(0-10)**|8|1|
|**skill-acquistion efficiency(0-10)**|8|2|
|**innate priors**|present|absent|

This post is inspired by [François Chollet's On the measure of intelligence](https://arxiv.org/pdf/1911.01547.pdf)
